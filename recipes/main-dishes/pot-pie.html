Veggie Pot Pie

Table of Contents

Ingredients

  • 2 tbs olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 8 ounces mushrooms (if big, slice)
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 large carrots, diced
  • 2 potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 2 stalks celery, peeled and sliced
  • 2 cups cauliflower florets
  • 1 cup green beans, cut into pieces
  • 3⁄4 cup vegetarian broth
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tbs cornstarch
  • 2 tbs soy sauce
  • 1 package Pillsbury crust (2 crusts in each)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F
  2. Heat oil in skillet. Cook onions, mushrooms and garlic in oil for 3-5 minutes, stirring frequently. Stir in carrots, potatoes, and celery. Stir in cauliflower, green beans, and vegetable broth. Bring to a boil, then turn heat down to a simmer. Cook until vegetables are tender, and cook until sauce thickens.
  3. In a small bowl, mix the cornstarch, soy sauce, and Â¼ cup water until cornstarch is completely dissolved. Stir into vegetables, and cook until sauce thickens, about 3 minutes.
  4. Put one pie dough in pie dish. Put filling on top. Put other dough on top and flute the edges.
  5. Bake for 30 minutes or until the crust is brown.

Spinach Dip

Table of Contents

Ingredients

  • 1 package (10 oz) frozen chopped spinach, thawed and well drained
  • 1 package cream cheese (8 oz), softened
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 4 green onions
  • 2 medium tomatoes, chopped

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients, cover tightly, and microwave on medium (50% power) for 9-12 minutes, stirring every 3 minutes until cheddar cheese is melted
  2. Serve with baguette or crackers

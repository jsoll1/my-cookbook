Beyond Beef Keema

Table of Contents

Ingredients

  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 5 tbs vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp black mustard seeds
  • 1 1⁄2 cup coarsely chopped onions
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 lb beyond beef
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 medium tomatoes, coarsely chopped
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup thawed frozen peas
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tbs lemon juice
  • pinch of dried mango powder (optional)
  • 2 tbs finely chopped fresh cilantro (for garnish)

Steps

  1. In a medium saucepan, heat oil over high heat until very hot but not smoking. Add cumin and mustard seeds, and cook stirring for 30 seconds. Reduce the heat to medium, add the onions, and cook, stirring occasionally, 10 minutes or until golden. Stir in the garlic and cook 1 minute.
  2. Add the bay leaves, cinnamon, turmeric, chili powder, ground cumin, ground coriander, and beyond beef, and cook, stirring occasionally, 10 minutes.
  3. Add the water, tomatoes, and salt, and cook 5 minutes.
  4. Reduce the heat to low, add the peas, and cook 10 minutes. Stir in the garam masala, lemon juice, and mango powder. Serve with fresh cilantro.
  5. Serve with rice.

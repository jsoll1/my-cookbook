Gnocchi with Mushrooms and Blue Cheese

Table of Contents

Ingredients

  • 2 of 400g packs of fresh gnocchi
  • 1 tbs olive oil
  • knob of butter
  • 1 large onion, roughly chopped
  • 500g small portobello mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 large garlic cloves, chopped
  • 150g pack creamy blue cheese (e.g. Danish Blue)
  • small pack parsley, chopped

Steps

  1. Bring a large pan of water to a boil and cook the gnocchi following pack instructions, Drain and set aside
  2. Meanwhile, heat the oil and butter in a large lidded frying pan. Add the onion and mushrooms, cook for 1 min over high heat, then turn down the heat to medium, put the lid on and cook for 5 minutes, stirring a few times.
  3. Remove the lid and add the garlic, cook for 1-2 mins, then stir the gnocchi into the pan. Scatter over blobs of cheese and the parsley.

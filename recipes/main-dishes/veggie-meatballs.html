Beyond Beef and Mushroom Meatballs

Table of Contents

Ingredients

  • 4 oz cremini mushroom (can substitute regular ones)
  • 1⁄2 small onion, chopped
  • 1⁄4 cup parsley leaves
  • 1 oz parmesan cheese, grated
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1⁄2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1⁄3 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1⁄4 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 lb beyond beef
  • 2 tbs olive oil
  • 1 24-oz jar or 3 cups marinara sauce

Steps

  1. Mix the following in a large bowl: mushrooms, onion, parsley, cheese and garlic. Add breadcrumbs, milk, egg, oregano, salt, and pepper. Mix lightly with a fork until just blended. Add beyond beef. Use your hands to mix until just combined.
  2. Form mixture in to 1 Â½ inch meatballs, wetting your hands with water to keep mixture from sticking. Place on a baking sheet so they are not toucan. You should have 16-18 meatballs.
  3. Heat olive oil in a saute pan over medium-high heat until simmering. Add meatballs in a single layer, working in batches if needed, and cook until browned on the bottom, 3 minutes. Flip meatballs and brown the other side, 3 minutes more. Transfer to large plate (the meatballs will not be fully cooked); repeat with remaining meatballs. Add marinara sauce and bring to a simmer. Gently add meatballs to sauce. Reduce heat to simmer gently until meatballs are cooked through, 10 minutes.
  4. TO BAKE THE MEATBALLS INSTEAD OF DOING IN PAN: Place uncooked meatballs on a rimmed baking sheet. Bake on the middle rack at 400F until lightly browned and cooked through, about 15 minutes.

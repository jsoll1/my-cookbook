Falafel

Table of Contents

Ingredients

  • 1 can chickpeas, drained
  • 6 scallions
  • 1 egg
  • 1⁄2 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 4 tbs chopped cilantro
  • 3 tbs mayonnaise
  • Salt, ground pepper
  • 1⁄4 cup olive oil
  • Pita bread

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F
  2. Put chickpeas in a blender. Add scallions and process to a smooth puree. Add egg, turmeric, garlic, cumin and 1 tbs of chopped cilantro. Process to mix. Season with salt and pepper to taste and mix.
  3. Put olive oil on sheet pan. Place spoonful of falafels on pan. Bake for 25 minutes, turning half way through.
  4. Stir in remaining chopped cilantro with mayonnaise.
  5. Serve falafel in pita bread with mayonnaise.

Rotelli with Walnut Sauce

Table of Contents

Ingredients

  • 1 lb rotelli or fusilli
  • 11⁄2 cups walnuts
  • 2 tbs butter
  • 3⁄4 tsp salt
  • 1⁄2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 3⁄4 cup olive oil
  • 1⁄2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1⁄2 cup heavy cream
  • 1⁄2 cup chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley

Steps

  1. Cook pasta according to instructions in boiling, salt water. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water. Drain.
  2. In a blender, combine walnuts, butter, salt, and pepper. Add in olive oil and. Blend. Transfer mixture to a small bowl and stir in the parmesan, and then the cream.
  3. Stir walnut sauce into pasta. Add the reserved pasta liquid, 1⁄4 cup at a time, until the sauce completely coats the pasta, using only as much as needed. Sprinkle with parsley and toss.

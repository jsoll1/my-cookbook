Guacamole

Table of Contents

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1 small onion, minced
  • 1 garlic clove, minced and mashed to a paste with 1⁄2 tsp salt
  • 4 tsp fresh lime juice or to taste
  • 1⁄2 tsp ground cumin
  • 3 tbs chopped cilantro, if desired

Steps

  1. Halve and pit the avacados and scoop the flesh into a bowl. Mash with a fork and stir in the onion, garlic paste, lime juice, cumin, and coriander. The guacamole may be made 2 hours in advance, its surface covered with plastic wrap and chilled.

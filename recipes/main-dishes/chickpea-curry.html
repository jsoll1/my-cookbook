Chickpea Curry

Table of Contents

Ingredients

  • 1 tbs vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbs garam masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 2 cups fresh tomato, diced
  • 2 cans chickpeas, drained
  • 1⁄2 cup water
  • 1⁄2 lemon, juiced
  • 1⁄4 cup chopped cilantro

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a medium pot over medium-high heat. Add onion and cook until translucent and begins to brown, about 3-5 minutes.
  2. Add garlic. Continue to cook for 3-4 minutes. Add garam masala, turmeric, salt, and pepper and continue to cook for 1-2 minutes.
  3. Add tomatoes, chickpeas, and water. Make sure to scrape any brown bits on the bottom or sides of the pot. Cover and cook for 15 minutes, while stirring occasionally.
  4. Remove lid, reduce heat to low and mix in the lemon juice and chopped cilantro. Cook over low heat for 1-2 minutes until the cilantro has wilted.

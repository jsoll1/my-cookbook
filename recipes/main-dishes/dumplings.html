Veggie Dumplings

Table of Contents

Ingredients

  • Filling
    • 1 zucchini
    • 1 carrot
    • 6 button mushrooms
    • 6 shiitake mushrooms
    • 4 green onions
    • 1⁄2 lb extra tofu
  • Seasoning
    • 2 tsp sesame seeds
    • 4 tbs soy sauce
    • 1⁄2 tsp ginger
    • 1 clove garlic, crushed
    • fash of hot sauce
    • 1 tbs sesame oil
    • vegetable oil
  • dumpling wrappers

Steps

  1. Chop all filling ingredients, finely.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in frying pan over high heat. Add carrot first and let cook for 1-2 minutes. Add remaining filling ingredients plus sesame seeds and cook 3-5 minutes until liquid is released and evaporating from veggies. Add the rest of the seasoning ingredients and cook until veggies are not liquified.
  3. Put about 1 tbs of filling in each dumpling and seal with water.
  4. Steam until the wrapper is somewhat translucent, about 10-15 minutes.

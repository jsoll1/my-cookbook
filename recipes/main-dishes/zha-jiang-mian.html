Ingredients

  • 1 pound beyond ground meat
  • 1 tbs canola oil
  • 3 shitake mushrooms, diced
  • 1 pound lo mein noodles or ramen noodles, cooked as instructed on package
  • 1 cup chili-garlic noodles sauce
  • 1⁄4 tsp red chili flake (optional)
  • Hoisin sauce to taste (just a glug)
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • Dash of ground black pepper
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 3 scallions, chopped small

Steps

  1. Heat oil in large frying pan over medium heat. Add beyond meat, shitake mushrooms, red chili flake, and cook. Break up with a wooden spoon for 5 minutes or until browned. Stir in the chili-garlic sauce, stirring for 2 minutes or until the meat is completely coated. Add the hoisin sauce to taste with sesame oil and ground pepper.
  2. Cook noodles as instructed. Drain. Divide among bowls with meat sauce. Then serve with cucumber and scallions.
  3. Add the water, tomatoes, and salt, and cook 5 minutes.
  4. Reduce the heat to low, add the peas, and cook 10 minutes. Stir in the garam masala, lemon juice, and mango powder. Serve with fresh cilantro.
  5. Serve with rice.

Wagon Wheels with Artichoke Pesto

Table of Contents

Ingredients

  • 1lb rotelle (wagon wheel pasta) or another kind of pasta
  • 8 oz can of artichoke hearts
  • 1 cup flat-leaf parsley
  • 1⁄2 cup chopped walnuts
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1⁄2 tsp salt
  • 1⁄2 tsp pepper
  • 3⁄4 cup olive oil
  • 2⁄3 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in a large pot of salted water, according to directions. Reserve 1⁄2 of the pasta cooking water. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a blender, combine artichokes, parsley, walnuts, lemon zest and juice, garlic, salt, and pepper. Add in olive oil to blender and run again. Transfer this to a serving bowl and stir in cheese. Add the warm pasta and toss to combine. If needed, add the reserved pasta water 1⁄4 cup at a time to create a more sauce-like consistency.

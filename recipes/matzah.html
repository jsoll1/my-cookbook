Matzah Balls (serves 20)

Table of Contents

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs, slightly beaten
  • 2 tbs vegetable oil
  • 1 cup matzah meal
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 2 tbs chopped parsley
  • 6 tbs vegetable broth or water
  • 4 quarts salted water

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, beat the eggs and the oil together. Stir in the matzah meal, salt, and parsley. Add the vegetable broth or water. Refrigerate 1 hour or more, to permit the meal to absorb the liquids.
  2. In a 6-quart poot with a lid, bring the slotted water to a boil. Reduce the water to a simmer and drop in balls of the matzah mixture about 1 Â½ inches in diameter. Cover the pot and cook just at a simmer for 20-40 minutes. When they are ready, they may be placed in soup to serve.

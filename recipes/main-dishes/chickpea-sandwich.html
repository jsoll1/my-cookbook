Chickpea Salad Sandwich

Table of Contents

Ingredients

  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1⁄4 cup red onion, diced
  • 1⁄2 red bell pepper, diced
  • 3 tbs mayonnaise
  • 1⁄2 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1⁄2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1⁄2 tsp onion powder
  • salt, to taste
  • pepper, to taste
  • 1 tbs fresh dill, chopped
  • leafy green, to serve
  • bread, sliced, to serve

Steps

  1. In medium mixing bowl, add chickpeas and mash with potato masher until a chunky texture is reached.
  2. Add the red onion, red pepper, mayo, mustard, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, pepper, and dill, and stir until well combined.
  3. Store chickpea salad in refrigerator for up to 5 days. Assemble sandwich.

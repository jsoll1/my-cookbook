Melted Brie

Table of Contents

Ingredients

  • 1 package puff pastry
  • 1 round of brie
  • raspberry preserves (seedless) or any jam

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Let pastry thaw 40 minutes to an hour
  3. Place brie on top of one sheet of pastry (there are 2)
  4. Put a thin layer of jam on top of brie
  5. Place a second sheet of pastry on top. Roll up and crimp edges with fingers
  6. Bake for 25 minutes and serve with crackers, apples, or sliced baguette.

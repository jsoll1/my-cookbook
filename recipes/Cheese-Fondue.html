Cheese Fondue

Table of Contents

Ingredients

  • 1 1⁄2 tbs olive oil
  • 3 scallions, finely sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 8 oz Emmenthaler cheese, grated
  • 8 oz gruyere cheese, grated
  • 1 cup sherry wine or dry white wine
  • 1 tbs cornstarch, mixed to a smooth paste with some water
  • grated nutmeg
  • 1⁄4 tsp cinnamon
  • paprika

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a pan and saute scallions and garlic for 2 minutes. Add wine or sherry and cornstarch paste. Then add the cheeses to the simmering wine, stir in a zig-zag motion to help break up the cheese.
  2. Season with salt, pepper, nutmeg, and cinnamon
  3. Heat gently stirring until melted and combined. If it is too thick, add some water to thin it down.
  4. Sprinkle with paprika
  5. Serve with apples, pears, celery, peppers, cubes of bread, or cooked potato skins.

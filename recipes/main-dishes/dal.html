Ingredients

  • 1 cup split red lentils
  • 3⁄4 cup finely chopped onions
  • 3 cups water
  • 1⁄2 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1⁄2 tsp ground cumin
  • 12 tsp ground coriander
  • 1⁄2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 1 can choppoed tomatoes
  • 2 tbs vegetable oil
  • 11⁄2 tsp salt

Steps

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine lentils, onions, water, turmeric, cumin, coriander, and chili powder. Heat to boiling, reduce the heat to low, and cook covered, 30 minutes.
  2. In a small skillet, heat butter and add garlic mixture and cook 2 minutes.
  3. Stir in the garlic mixture, chopped tomatoes, oil, and salt into the lentils. Cook, covered 30 minutes.
  4. Serve with rice.

Veggie Spring Rolls

Table of Contents

Ingredients

  • 8 spring roll rice paper wraps
  • 1 avocado pitted and cut into 8 slices
  • 3 cups salad greens
  • 2 cups carrots, shredded
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups cabbage (red, green, or Napa), shredded
  • 1 cucumber cut into skinny stripes
  • 1⁄2 cup fresh mint leaves
  • 1⁄2 cup fresh cilantro leaves
  • 1⁄2 cup fresh basil or Thai basil leaves
  • 1⁄2 cup green onions, sliced
  • hot water
  • crushed peanuts for garnish

Steps

  1. Fill a large bowl with warm water (large enough to fit the rice paper wrappers in flatly). Dip a wrapper for 5-10 seconds to soften, then lay it flat on a plate.
  2. Place one piece of avocado, salad greens, carrot, cabbage, cucumber, bell pepper, and fresh herbs horizontally on one end of the wrapper.
  3. Place a 10-inch non-stick pan or a cast-iron skillet over low heat to preheat. Divide dough in 4 parts. Cover dough when set aside. Take one piece. Roll into thin round on lightly floured surface. Use pastry brush to spread oil-flour mixture on surface.
  4. Fold ends inwards and roll up.
  5. Serve with Sweet Chili-Peanut Sauce

Sweet Chili-Peanut Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1⁄2 cup peanut butter (chunky or smooth), at room temperature
  • 1⁄2 cup sweet chili sauce (not paste)
  • 1 tbs soy sauce
  • 1 tbs water

Steps

  1. Combine the peanut butter, sweet chili sauce, and soy sauce in a small bowl with a whisk or spoon.
  2. Add a small amount of water for a thinner consistency.
  3. Sprinkle the peanuts on top for garnish.
  4. Keep any unused portion in an airtight container for up to 2 weeks in the refrigerator
